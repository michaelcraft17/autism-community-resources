Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our directory and autism resources

← Back to Directory About Submit Resource
How do I find resources in my area? ▼
Use the search bar to enter your city, state, or zip code. You can also search for specific services like "speech therapy" or "support groups." Our filters help you narrow down results by resource type and age group.
What types of resources are included in the directory? ▼
Our directory includes:
  • Advocacy organizations and support groups
  • Therapy services (speech, occupational, behavioral)
  • Educational support and special education resources
  • Medical and diagnostic services
  • Early intervention programs
  • Employment and vocational training
  • Respite care services
  • Assistive technology resources
How often is the directory updated? ▼
We regularly review and update our listings to ensure accuracy. However, we recommend verifying contact information and services directly with providers, as details can change frequently.
How can I add a resource to the directory? ▼
Use our resource submission form to suggest new organizations or services. We review all submissions before adding them to the directory.
Are all resources free? ▼
No, our directory includes both free and fee-based services. Each listing includes information about insurance accepted and payment options where available. Many services accept Medicaid or private insurance.
What should I do if I find incorrect information? ▼
Please let us know about any inaccuracies through our submission form. Include the resource name and the correct information, and we'll update our records.
Can I use this directory on my mobile phone? ▼
Yes! Our directory is designed to work on all devices - smartphones, tablets, and computers. The layout automatically adjusts to your screen size for easy browsing.
Do you provide services directly? ▼
No, we are a directory service that connects families with resources. We do not provide direct services like therapy or support groups. You'll need to contact the organizations listed for their services.
What if I can't find what I'm looking for? ▼
Try broadening your search terms or contact us with your specific needs. We may be able to suggest resources not yet in our directory or help you find alternatives in nearby areas.
