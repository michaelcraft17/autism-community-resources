Our Mission

The Autism & Special Needs Community Directory was created to bridge the gap between families seeking support and the valuable resources available in their communities. We understand that navigating the world of autism and special needs services can be overwhelming, and our goal is to make that journey easier.

What We Offer

🔍 Comprehensive Search

Find resources by location, service type, age group, and specific needs. Our advanced filtering helps you discover exactly what you're looking for.

📋 Detailed Information

Each resource listing includes contact information, services offered, age groups served, insurance accepted, and languages spoken.

🌐 Always Current

We regularly update our database to ensure accuracy and add new resources as they become available in the community.

📱 Mobile Friendly

Resource Categories

Our directory includes a wide range of services and support options:

  • Advocacy Organizations: Groups that fight for rights and provide information
  • Therapy Services: Speech, occupational, physical, and behavioral therapy
  • Educational Support: Special education advocacy, IEP support, tutoring
  • Medical Services: Diagnosis, treatment, and specialized medical care
  • Social Support: Support groups, social skills training, community events
  • Early Intervention: Services for children ages 0-3 with developmental delays
  • Employment Services: Job training and support for adults with autism
  • Respite Care: Temporary relief care for families and caregivers
  • Assistive Technology: Tools and devices to support communication and learning

How to Use This Directory

Start by using the search bar to look for specific services or locations. Use the filters to narrow down results by resource type or age group. Click on any resource card to view detailed information, and use the action buttons to call or visit their website directly.
